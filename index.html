Welcome

With 12 years of experience in computational finance and AI, I lead with a hands-on approach to developing and deploying advanced quantitative models. My work integrates machine learning, stochastic processes, and complex risk management frameworks to address critical challenges in finance.

I specialize in the calibration and application of models such as the Heston model, Hull-White interest rate models, and the Black-Karasinski model, tailoring them to meet the precise demands of risk management and market risk.

My recent projects include FRTB simulation frameworks, tail risk hedging, and Bayesian inference models for predictive analytics. Explore my work and research to discover how AI and finance converge to shape tomorrow's markets.
