Welcome

With over a decade of expertise as a lead scientist in computational finance and AI, I bring a deep commitment to advancing quantitative finance through innovative modeling, AI-driven research, and rigorous risk management. My work spans diverse, high-impact projects, from option pricing and scenario simulation for tail risk hedging to developing AI-based methods for climate and green finance.

Specializing in quantitative trading strategies, interest rate risk, and credit valuation adjustments, my focus is on combining AI and financial theory to solve complex market challenges. Leveraging advanced techniques like Bayesian machine learning, Heston model calibration, and dynamic hedging, I aim to push the boundaries of financial research and practice.
