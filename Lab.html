Quant Trading Research Lab

The Quant Trading Research Lab()QFCM Lab, Bootstrap(2020- Feb 2025) is dedicated to developing, validating, and deploying cutting-edge quantitative models and trading strategies across multi-asset portfolios. Leveraging advanced stochastic processes, Monte Carlo simulations, and machine learning, the lab focuses on market risk analytics, derivatives pricing, and dynamic hedging under realistic macroeconomic and stress scenarios. Our work supports risk-adjusted alpha generation while aligning with regulatory frameworks such as IFRS 9, FRTB, and IRRBB.
