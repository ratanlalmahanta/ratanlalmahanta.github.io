Research & Development Projects

AI-Driven Financial Markets

Focused on integrating advanced AI methodologies to enhance financial market predictions, risk management, and decision-making. This includes building quantitative models that leverage machine learning for real-time analysis and scenario simulations.

QuantEquity Selection Model

Developed a data-driven equity selection model, employing machine learning algorithms and risk management strategies. The model is designed for dynamic sector-wise stock selection using an ML approach for optimal portfolio construction.

Heston Model Calibration for Volatility

Worked on calibrating the Heston model to fit market volatility quotes, focusing on achieving precise calibration for one-year maturity options. The project compares various model parameters to ensure alignment with real-world data.

Credit Valuation Adjustment (CVA) Computation

Developed a robust method for calculating Credit Valuation Adjustment (CVA), incorporating Monte Carlo simulations and quantitative techniques to manage counterparty risk in credit derivatives.

Hull-White Short-Rate Model Calibration

Explored interest rate modeling by calibrating the Hull-White model with a focus on parameters 'a' and 'sigma'. This project integrates QuantLib and Monte Carlo simulations to address credit risk modeling through real-world case studies.

Fundamental Review of the Trading Book (FRTB) Implementation

Implemented the FRTB Standard Approach with applications to FX and interest rate asset classes. This involved complex mathematical derivations and modeling techniques, aimed at meeting regulatory requirements and mitigating market risks.

Green Finance & Climate Risk Modeling

Part of a Special Issue paper submission focused on novel mathematical and statistical approaches to address climate risk and promote sustainable finance. Research integrates AI-based methods to optimize policies and financial products for environmental transition.

Quantitative Trading Strategies

Developed advanced trading strategies involving technical analysis, neural networks, and log-optimal portfolio construction. This project addresses asset forecasting and portfolio optimization through quantitative methods.
