Key Research Areas

AI in Quantitative Finance

My research primarily focuses on integrating AI into financial market models, especially in predictive analytics and real-time trading systems. This includes developing machine learning models for algorithmic trading, dynamic hedging, and portfolio optimization.

Tail Risk Hedging & Market Risk Models

I've worked on creating risk management frameworks that account for tail risks and extreme market events. This includes the application of stochastic volatility models, Monte Carlo simulations, and machine learning-based dynamic risk assessment techniques.

Climate Risk & Green Finance

Research in green finance has been a key area of focus, with an emphasis on developing financial instruments and policies to support the transition to a sustainable economy. I have also explored the integration of climate risk into financial portfolios and the development of optimal financial policies to mitigate climate impact.
