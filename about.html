Welcome!

I’m Ratanlal Mahanta, a dedicated researcher and lead scientist specializing in Computational Finance and AI-driven Quantitative Research. With 12+ years of experience in model development and validation, I build and audit advanced pricing, risk, and trading models across asset classes. My work spans from stochastic volatility models, machine learning in risk management, to structured product pricing and CVA/xVA engines.

I contribute to regulatory capital frameworks (FRTB, SA-CCR, IRRBB), lead validator–developer integrations, and design intelligent validation platforms for next-gen risk and valuation engines. Passionate about fintech innovation, I continuously explore cross-domain applications of AI in trading, liquidity risk, repo markets, and derivatives.
